Maharashtra's Rich Historical Tapestry

Tracing its Ancient Roots to Modern Identity

Maharashtra's history spans from ancient civilizations like Satavahanas and Vakatakas, establishing early cultural foundations. It evolved through the Yadavas, fostering Marathi language, medieval sultanates, the Maratha Empire, and British rule, culminating in state formation in 1960.
