AI Agent Vs Agentic AI

AI Agent vs. Agentic AI: The Evolution

Defining Fundamental Concepts

🤖

AI Agent

  • Foundational concept, dating back decades.
  • Perceives its environment and acts upon it.
🧠

Agentic AI

  • Recent evolution, driven by advanced LLMs.
  • Represents higher autonomy and self-direction.
  • All Agentic AI are AI Agents; not all AI Agents are Agentic.
