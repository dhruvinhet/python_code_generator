AI Agent Vs Agentic AI

Key Differences: Autonomy, Planning & Goals

Beyond Traditional AI Agents

Characteristic ⚙️ AI Agent 🧠 Agentic AI
🧍 Autonomy Executes predefined tasks, often rule-based/reactive. Operates within a circumscribed scope, simple goals. Higher autonomy, initiative, self-direction.
🗺️ Planning Simple, direct execution based on immediate perception. Complex goal decomposition, multi-step planning, tool use. Self-corrects, uses recursive reasoning, adapts.
🎯 Goals Simple, explicit, short-term. Sophisticated reasoning, leverages LLMs for complex, long-term goals.
