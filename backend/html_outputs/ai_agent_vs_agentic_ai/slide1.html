AI Agent vs. Agentic AI: An Introduction

Defining core concepts and distinction

⚙️

AI Agent

  • Perceives environment, acts to achieve goals.
  • Reactive or simple proactive behavior.
  • Operates within defined parameters.
VS
🧠

Agentic AI

  • High agency, advanced planning capabilities.
  • Multi-step execution towards complex objectives.
  • Enabled by powerful Large Language Models.
Evolution: From reactive to proactive, goal-driven systems
